I tried to fly away but found

myself remaining near the ground.

Felix sighed. "I'd hoped you'd stay.

Of your own will. But anyway.

I watch as felix stiffened, fell.

"You see, although it's hard to tell

I'm already inside your ship!

I found a way through which to slip.

First things first, your weapons suck.

You must be blessed with ample luck.

Your flash is dim, your din is dumb

I'll fix you up with hybrid scum

that settled in your steel pores

will form a strata for new spores

to root into your neural net.

We'll be the fiercest fighter yet!"
