We burned across mountain range

full of hostile proto apes

a strangely retro hybrid spawn

like we once were, in mannish shapes

"Those blasted brains!" our Felix crowed,

"We'll eat them up, a mighty bite!

My yarling call, an icy stab

A split of terror through the night.

Our form had grown since we had joined

A vast expanse of screaming sky

No longer did we fear the grot

The scimp, the gnarling gnerps, the prie.
